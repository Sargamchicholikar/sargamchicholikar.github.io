📸 Drive Photo Classifier

🔒 Your photos never leave your device

Drive Photo Classifier is a Chrome extension that automatically organises your Google Drive photos into folders — Animals, People, Groups, and Junk — using an on-device AI model.

How it works

✅ Connect your Google Drive account
✅ The AI classifies each photo locally inside your browser
✅ Photos are moved into organised folders in your Drive
✅ No photos are ever sent to any third-party server

Why we need Google Drive access

The extension requests access to your Google Drive solely to:

  • Read your photos for on-device classification
  • Create organised folders in your Drive
  • Move photos into the appropriate folders

We do not store, share, or transmit your photos or personal data to any server.

Install

Get it on the Chrome Web Store
